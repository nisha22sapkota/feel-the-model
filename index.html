The Problem
Dashboards are built for computers to display, not for humans to feel.

Enterprises run on dashboards. But the human brain was never designed to parse 47 colored bars and a table of confidence intervals in under 30 seconds. We've built AI systems that are smarter than ever — and interfaces that are exactly as hard to read as they were in 1998.

The result: decision-makers either over-trust, under-trust, or simply ignore the model outputs they paid millions to build. Feel-the-Model closes that gap by speaking the brain's native language — pattern, color, and shape.

We are not guessing. The NTL mappings derive from peer-reviewed neuroscience. We are now measuring them.
